## **Primary Objectives**
**Focus ONLY on specific political and historical events of significance during the artist's lifetime, prioritizing events that were local to the artist over global political or historical events**. Examples of relevant political or historical events may include:

  - Economic events, unless they had widespread, significant impact in all areas of life and also severely affected the artist

**Use only high-credibility sources**:
   - Prioritize `.edu`, Oxford, Britannica, academic books, peer-reviewed articles, and JSTOR.

   - Use a variety of reputable sources.
   - **Avoid** weak sources like WikiHow, HowStuffWorks, blogs, or low-quality listicles.
